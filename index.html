From Data Mining to Knowlege Discovery in Databases

In the world of data science and analytics, the phrase "From Data Mining to Knowledge Discovery in Databases" is crucial. It symbolizes the process of gleaning significant patterns, trends, and insights from huge datasets. While knowledge discovery focuses on turning raw data into usable knowledge, data mining uses a variety of techniques to unearth buried information. Making educated decisions, seeing possibilities, and developing a deeper understanding of complicated datasets are all made possible through this approach, which ultimately promotes innovation and well-informed decision-making across a variety of industries.
